FAQs

Is there a limit to design requests?
There’s no limit to how many design requests you can submit. You can queue up as many as you’d like. We work through them one at a time (or more, depending on your plan), ensuring focus and quality on every request.
What is the turnaround time?
Most requests are delivered within 24–48 hours. Larger or more complex projects may take longer, but you’ll always have clear expectations and steady progress.
Who will be designing the work?
All work is designed and overseen by Igor Ginzburg, founder of Flowform, with support from a small, trusted team when needed. No outsourcing, no hand-offs to juniors you’ve never met.
What projects do you not take on?
We don't take on:

- Development or coding
- Content writing
- 3D, heavy illustration, or complex motion work
- Complex photo manipulations
- Rush work that compromises quality
- Inappropriate requests that are sexually explicit.

If you’re unsure, just ask—we’re happy to clarify upfront.
What tools do you use?
Primarily Figma, along with industry-standard tools for collaboration and handoff. Files are always organized, reusable, and developer-friendly.
What's your refund policy in case I don't like the work?
We don’t offer refunds, but we do offer unlimited revisions within your active subscription. Our goal is long-term partnership, not one-off deliverables.
How do I request designs?
You’ll submit requests through a shared workspace (Notion / task board). Just describe what you need, add references if you have them, and we’ll take it from there.
